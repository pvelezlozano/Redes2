In 2013, drummer Jason Barnes worked with researchers at Georgia Tech to build the world’s most advanced robotic drumming arm. Their latest design uses Google’s open source machine learning platform, Tensorflow, to help him play intuitively, almost as if he were playing with his own arm. Learn more → goo.gle/forwardrhythm
